Professional Summary

Dr. Md. Yousuf Kabir is a leading Specialist Thoracic and Esophageal surgeon in Dhaka with over 5+ years of experience in advanced chest and lung surgeries. He completed his MBBS and specialized in Thoracic Surgery (MS,NIDCH, Mohakhali), with further training in minimally invasive VATS surgery. He is a consultant thoracic surgeon at Dhaka Medical College Hospital and affiliated with cancer treatment centers in Bangladesh. His expertise includes lung cancer surgery, chest trauma care, pleural diseases, and chest wall reconstruction.Dr. Kabir is a member of the Bangladesh Society of Thoracic Surgeons, fluent in Bengali, English, and Hindi, and is known for his patient-centered, compassionate approach.
